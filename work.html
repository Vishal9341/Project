Simple Three-Step Process

From food donation to community distribution, we make it easy to make a difference

List Surplus Food

Restaurants and food businesses list available surplus food on our platform with details and pickup time.

Smart Matching

Our system instantly notifies nearby NGOs about available donations based on their capacity and needs.

Delivery & Distribution

NGOs collect the food and distribute it to communities in need through their established networks.

Step 1: Easy Food Listing

Food providers can quickly list surplus food through our user-friendly platform or mobile app. The process takes less than 2 minutes.

  • Specify food type, quantity, and preparation time
  • Set preferred pickup windows
  • Upload photos for transparency
  • Get instant confirmation of listing

Our system automatically categorizes food items and ensures they meet safety standards before being shared with partner NGOs.

Step 2: Intelligent Matching

Our smart algorithm matches food donations with the most suitable NGOs based on multiple factors to ensure efficient distribution.

  • Location-based matching for quick pickup
  • Capacity matching based on NGO size
  • Preference matching for specific food types
  • Real-time notifications via app and SMS

NGOs receive instant alerts about new donations and can claim them with a single click, ensuring minimal response time.

Step 3: Efficient Distribution

Once an NGO claims a donation, they coordinate pickup and distribute the food to communities through their established networks.

  • Scheduled pickup to minimize disruption
  • Temperature-controlled transport when needed
  • Distribution tracking and reporting
  • Impact feedback to donors

Donors receive reports showing how their contribution made a difference, creating a transparent and rewarding experience.
